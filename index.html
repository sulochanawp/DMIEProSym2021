About Us෴

Manufacturing Engineering Association(MEA) is an university registered society comprising of academic staff and students of the department. MEA focuses on developing students' technical knowledge as well as soft skills by organizing various events throughout the year. The raised connection between the academic staff and the students via MEA leads the department on the correct path to achieve the desired outcome.
