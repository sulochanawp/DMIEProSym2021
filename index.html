About Us

Manufacturing Engineering Association(MEA) is an university registered society comprising of academic staff and students of the department. MEA focuses on developing students' technical knowledge as well as soft skills by organizing various events throughout the year. The raised connection between the academic staff and the students via MEA leads the department on the correct path to achieve the desired outcome.

Events


INDUSTRIO 2021

INDUSTRIO is the project symposium of the Department of Manufacturing and Industrial Engineering of The University of Peradeniya. It presents the projects of the final year undergraduates of the department.

Webinar Series

The webinar series is organized by MEA to lend a hand to students of the department to improve their soft skills and to emphasize personal branding.

MEA CAD Competition

MEA CAD Competition is organized by the Manufacturing Engineering Association in collaboration with SIEMENS Digital Industries for all engineering undergraduates of the faculty of engineering University of Peradeniya.

MEA Workshop

CAD webinar series is organized by MEA for second-year engineering undergraduates of the faculty to deliver awareness about 3D modeling and designing as well as to sharpen up the knowledge of CAD modeling.
